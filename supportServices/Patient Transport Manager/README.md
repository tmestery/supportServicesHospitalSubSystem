### **Use Case: Manage Patient Transport Requests**

**Trigger:**
A patient requires transportation within the hospital (e.g., to radiology, surgery, or another department).

**Actor:**
Health Information Technology (HIT) Employee

**Preconditions:**

* HIT employees are logged into the hospital transport management system.
* Patient records and transport scheduling systems are accessible.
* Transport staff are available and contactable.

**Main Success Scenario (Basic Flow):**

1. HIT employee receives a transport request from a nurse, doctor, or department.
2. Verifies patient details and transport requirements (e.g., *wheelchair*, *stretcher*, *isolation precautions*).
3. Schedules transport in the system, assigning available transport staff.
4. Notifies transport staff of the assignment and confirms estimated pickup time.
5. Updates the system once transport is completed.

**Outcome:**

* The patient is safely transported to the required location.
* Transport requests are logged and completed in the system.
* Hospital workflow and patient care are efficiently maintained.
