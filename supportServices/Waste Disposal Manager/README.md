### **Use Case: Manage Hospital Waste Disposal**

**Trigger:**
Waste collection schedule or immediate disposal requirement.

**Actor:**
Cleaning Staff

**Preconditions:**

* Cleaning staff is logged into the hospital waste management system (if applicable).
* Staff has access to waste disposal tools and containers.
* Waste segregation guidelines are known and available.

**Main Success Scenario (Basic Flow):**

1. Cleaning staff identifies the type and location of waste (e.g., *biohazard*, *general*, *recyclable*).
2. Collects waste using appropriate containers and follows safety procedures.
3. Updates the system (or logbook) with the type, quantity, and disposal location of the waste.
4. Transports waste to the designated disposal area (e.g., *incinerator*, *recycling*, *hazardous storage*).
5. Confirms disposal completion in the system or logbook.

**Outcome:**

* Waste is safely collected, segregated, and disposed of according to regulations.
* Records of waste disposal are accurately updated.
* Compliance with hospital and legal safety standards is maintained.
