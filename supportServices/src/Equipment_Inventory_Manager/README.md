### **Use Case: Manage Medical Equipment Inventory & Repairs**

**Trigger:**
Equipment requires maintenance, repair, or an inventory update.

**Actor:**
Equipment Technician

**Preconditions:**

* Technician is logged into the inventory management system.
* Equipment records already exist in the system.
* Technician has access rights to update inventory and repair records.

**Main Success Scenario (Basic Flow):**

1. Technician logs into the inventory management system.
2. Searches for the equipment that needs maintenance or repair.
3. Updates the equipment status (e.g., *working*, *under repair*, *out of service*).
4. Records details of any repairs performed or parts replaced.
5. Updates inventory if parts are consumed or added.
6. Submits the updated record.

**Outcome:**

* Equipment status and repair history are updated in the system.
* Inventory reflects accurate stock levels.
* Technician receives confirmation of a successful update.
