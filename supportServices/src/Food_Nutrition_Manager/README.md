### **Use Case: Manage Food & Nutrition Delivery**

**Trigger:**
Patient meal schedule or dietary order update.

**Actor:**
Administrative Dieticians

**Preconditions:**

* Dietician is logged into the hospital food management system.
* Patient dietary requirements and restrictions are available.
* Kitchen staff and delivery personnel are available.

**Main Success Scenario (Basic Flow):**

1. Dietician reviews patients' dietary needs and meal schedules.
2. Creates or updates meal orders according to patient restrictions and preferences.
3. Sends meal orders to the kitchen for preparation.
4. Coordinates with delivery staff to ensure timely distribution to patient rooms.
5. Confirms meal delivery and updates the system to reflect completion.

**Outcome:**

* Patients receive meals that meet their dietary requirements.
* Food delivery records are accurately maintained.
* Hospital nutritional care standards are upheld.
